How BRAINIC Helps You

Alzheimer's disease and brain tumors are leading causes of cognitive decline and mortality worldwide. Early diagnosis is crucial for effective treatment, yet traditional methods of analyzing MRI scans are slow, prone to human error, and require specialized medical expertise, which is not always accessible, especially in underserved regions. This project aims to address these challenges by developing an AI-powered tool that automates the analysis of MRI scans, providing faster, more accurate diagnoses. By leveraging Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), the system will classify MRI scans into categories such as Alzheimer's disease, brain tumors, or normal, helping healthcare professionals make quicker and more reliable diagnostic decisions, ultimately improving patient outcomes.
